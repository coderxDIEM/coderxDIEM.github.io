Korea Trip


Recently I have been to Korea for 37 days of which exactly 26 days were my summer programme at Hanyang University (located in Seoul). I find the city of Korea to be quite similar to Singapore in my aspects. For instance, Seoul is a fast pace city, where you will be able to see many Koreans rushing from one place to another (especially in the subway). Next, due to the high cost of living (Accommodation, Transport & Food) in Seoul, the stressful life has led to many Koreans picking up the habit of smoking and drinking at night.

On the last 9 days of my trip in Korea, I took KTX to Busan (stayed for 3 days), followed by Daegu via Mugunghwa (stayed for 2 days) and finally back in Seoul via ITX for last minute shopping.

Among the three cities, I feel that I enjoyed myself the most when I was in Seoul, as it is more vibrant and lively throughout the day. What I like most about Busan is their beaches. Lastly, Daegu is nice only if you are a natural lover. However, be warned not to visit this city during the Summer! It is scorching hot (since there were no clouds blocking the sun), you will definitely get a sunburn after the trip!
